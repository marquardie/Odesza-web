Areas of Work

What the Cards Can Illuminate

Tarot is a mirror, not a fortune machine. Here's where it speaks most clearly.

Love & Relationships

Tarot cuts through projection and fear to reveal what's truly happening between you and another. Best for: situationships, breakups, new connections, self-worth in love.

Career & Life Path

Clarity on what you already know but haven't admitted yet. Tarot surfaces the options, blocks, and timing your rational mind avoids. Best for: career crossroads, business decisions, burnout.

Self-Discovery

Tarot reads the parts of you that haven't found words yet — patterns, gifts, wounds, potential. Best for: feeling lost, identity shifts, inner work, recurring life themes.

Energetic Blocks & Shadow Work

Shadow work through tarot names what's hiding beneath the surface — the belief, the fear, the pattern. Best for: self-sabotage, repeating situations, emotional heaviness.

Important Decisions

A third perspective — not yours, not others', but something quieter and more honest. Best for: major life choices, moving, ending or starting something significant.

Timing & What's Coming

Tarot reads the current energy and where it's heading. Sometimes that's enough to know what to do right now. Best for: "is this the right time?", seasonal readings, energy forecasts.
